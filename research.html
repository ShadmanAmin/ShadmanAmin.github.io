My work sits where ecohydrology, remote sensing and applied engineering overlap. In practice that means I design and deploy the instruments that generate my data, then process the result on UCSB's HPC cluster. The fieldwork and the computation are the same project; treating them separately is how you end up with a beautiful pipeline fed by a sensor that stopped reporting in March.
